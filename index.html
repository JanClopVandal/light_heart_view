Interactive Light Installation

Light Heart

About

Light Heart is an interactive installation that addresses the image of a tower built in the shape of a heart — a symbol of unification, light and joint action.

Technology
& Implementation

The central figure is assembled from LED screens with integrated light scattering panels. The structure is placed on a pedestal, and infrared sensors are installed around the perimeter of the hall, tracking the movement of the audience.

The screens display generative graphics inspired by the visual palette of the Tower project. At the base of the object there is a running line on which messages sent by viewers via the Telegram bot appear. After moderation, the texts are projected onto the screen and symbolically sent to the “heart”.

Interaction

The installation works in three states:

(1)
In standby mode, it shows generative graphics and a quiet glow.
(2)
As the audience approaches, visual and light dynamics are activated, creating a live response effect.
(3)
Through the Telegram bot, viewers can send their messages, which become part of the overall light stream.
